Store Policies

SHIPPING INFORMATION

All orders are processed within 10 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email as soon as your package is dispatched.

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 Business Days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 Business Days
RETURNS & SIZE ISSUES 48-Hour Window

We offer a strict 2-day (48-hour) return/exchange window from the time of delivery for sizing issues or manufacturing defects.

How to proceed:
  1. Contact us at support@Fuisell.com within 48 hours of delivery.
  2. Provide your order number and a photo of the item/tag.
  3. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging.

Note: Requests made after the 48-hour window or items marked "Final Sale" are not eligible for return or refund.
